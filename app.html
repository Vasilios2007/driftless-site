The app

Most apps want your time. Driftless hands it back.

Built for maladaptive daydreaming — but it's for anyone who drifts. Every session is designed to end, so you leave lighter and more here, not pulled deeper in.

🌅

Land in the present

Open to a breathing sun and a gentle daily affirmation. A 30-second check-in helps you notice where you are — no pressure, no streaks.

🌱

Watch yourself grow

Every grounding win plants something in a calm, living garden. Progress as gentle growth — never a score, never a leaderboard.

🫂

People who get it

An anonymous community oriented around feelings and coping — not fantasy plots. Supportive-only reactions. No public like-counts.
