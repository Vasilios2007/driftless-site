Why it's different

“Connection without reinforcement. A community of daydreamers can deepen the habit by glamorizing the fantasy — so here, everything orients to you, not the plot.”
Sessions that end. Finite feeds reach a visible “you're all caught up.” No autoplay, no bottomless scroll.
Gentle, not gamified. No streak-shaming, no public counts, no leaderboards. Notifications are opt-in and capped at one a day.
Safety first. Peer support — not therapy. Crisis resources are always reachable, surfaced supportively.

The whole idea

We measure success by you needing us less.

No metric here rewards time-in-app. The win is you closing it and going to live a little.
