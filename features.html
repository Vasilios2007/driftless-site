Features

Small, thoughtful tools — nothing that hooks you.

Everything here is built to ease the pull, not feed it.

☀️

Breathe

A radiant sun that breathes with you — a quiet grounding ritual any time you need it.

🌿

Your garden

Wins and intentions grow a living scene that's yours alone. Private, encouraging, never shaming.

✍️

Structured sharing

A gentle composer keeps the community about the human and the experience — feelings, triggers, coping — not the fantasy.

🌙

Dream space

An opt-in, private corner for the daydreams themselves. Off by default, never in the feed.

🔒

Yours, anonymously

No real name, no contacts, no location. Minimal data, easy deletion. You're a drifter, not a profile.

🧰

Grounding toolkit

Tiny, one-step-at-a-time actions to come back now, reconnect, prevent the pull, and refocus.

Driftless is peer support, not professional care. If you're struggling, talking to a professional can help. In the US, call or text 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), or find a helpline near you at findahelpline.com.
